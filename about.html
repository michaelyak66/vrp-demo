About Us

Accelerating growth for businesses in the downstream energy sector

OyaSync is an ecosystem of innovative solutions targeted at accelerating the growth of businesses in the oil and gas sector through tools for profit maximization, generation and access to low-interest credit.

We are committed to providing top-notch service and support to our clients. Our team of experts is always available to assist with any questions or challenges, ensuring that our solutions are tailored to meet your specific needs.

At OyaSync, our mission is to simplify and enhance the operations of Gas and Fuel businesses. We aim to provide an integrated ecosystem that streamlines inventory management, sales reporting, cash handling, and financial support.

Our vision is to be the leading catalyst for transformation in the downstream energy sector, driving innovation and growth by enabling seamless value chain integration and access to affordable credit.

We envision a future where over 1500 businesses and 2,500,000 customers thrive through our platform, creating a robust ecosystem of new opportunities and sustainable development across the energy value chain.

Empowering Gas and Fuel Businesses with Innovative Solutions

We are a solutions company dedicated to providing comprehensive and innovative solutions for businesses in the downstream energy sector. With the VRP we have followed the trends of demand and supply which has influenced our investments in the sector to enhance the quality of data we collect.

Our mission is to empower gas plant owners and distributors with the tools and resources they need to thrive in a competitive market.


Providing Funding for Gas and Fuel Operations

Our access to real-time data in the gas distribution value chain, coupled with OyaSync's thriving and successful Value Chain Resource Platform (OSVRP) with multiple LPG and CNG gas players, has revealed the necessity for a Funding SPV to ensure derisked investor participation through access to real-time data of value chain players who can further organize and enhance the downstream sector using technology.

Currently, we manage transactions of over N60bn annually which we believe has the potential to access institutional funding to drive the gas value chain to meet its market potential across Nigeria. ‎ .
